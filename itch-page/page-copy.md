## Description

### About

**chronost** is a compact, single-screen time-loop puzzle platformer.

You have ten seconds. When the countdown ends, your movement becomes a ghost that repeats everything you did. Build a team of past selves, leave one on the switch, open the door, and get your current self to the goal.

End a loop early when the recording is right. If the timeline gets tangled, clear it and begin again. The whole puzzle stays in view, so every solution is about timing, positioning, and cooperation with yourself.

Made for GMTK Game Jam 2026 and its theme, **COUNT DOWN**.

### Controls

- **A / D** or **Left / Right:** Move
- **Space**, **W**, or **Up:** Jump
- **R:** End the current loop and create a ghost
- **C:** Reset the timeline and clear all ghosts

### Browser Notes

Desktop keyboard recommended. Click the game once to focus it. Touch controls are not currently supported.

If a browser extension captures single-key shortcuts, disable its bindings for this page while playing.
